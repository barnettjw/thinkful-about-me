Introduction

I got my start working on a helpdesk and worked my way to being a Systems Administrator. Working in technical support is where I learned about satisfying it is to help people solve their problems.

My love of building things on the web started when I was setting up a network monitoring system at work and my boss asked if we could put it on a TV so our customers could see it when they came to visit the datacenter. So I created a custom dashboard and that was my first exposure to CSS.

Since then I've been hooked on the power to come up with an idea and actually build it myself.

I'm passionate about good documentation, great UX and helping people solve their own problems.

Where I'm From
